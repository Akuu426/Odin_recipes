egg sauce recipe

Description

A sweet low heat made egg sauce to be eaten with either of yam, rice, sweet potato. can also be taken with bread as breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 4 raw eggs
  • 10 fresh tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 chilli pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 onion bulb, chopped
  • 1 green pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 seasoning cube
  • 1/8 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/4 tsp curry
  • salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil

Steps

  1. break the eggs into a plate
  2. place a pan on heat and pour in the vegetable oil
  3. when the oil is hot, add the chopped onions and stir
  4. add the chopped tomatoes and pepper and fry for about 4 mins
  5. add the chopped green pepper
  6. pour in the egg and allow it to set for about 30secs
  7. sprinkle the seasoning cube, curry and thyme and stir the egg mixture
  8. add salt to taste and serve as desired
